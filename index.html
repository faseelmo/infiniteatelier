ABOUT US

Infinit Atelier is a multi-disciplinary design practice, started in 2015 and driven by expertise, design knowledge and innovation. At Infinit Atelier , we pursue Architecture, Interior Design, and Furniture Design with sheer commitment to artful composition. Our team of proficient architects & designers strive to create tasteful and tailor-made personal spaces for our clients.

Our design practice scales the spectrum of retail design, F & B projects, office spaces as well as residential projects. We believe in elegance, the creation of holistic spaces and designing effectively using simple elements. From conceptualization to execution, our focus is on perfecting the details and transforming voids into livable spaces.
